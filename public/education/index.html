Education

Prova Lab offers single-day intensive courses (our Bootcamps) and one-hour workshops on a range of impact- and innovation-related topics.

Bootcamps

We’ve created a unique instructional design that allows for a fun, interactive cohort environment. We specialize in foundational courses aimed at demystifying the concepts of social innovation and social impact. Bootcamps are conducted in one day. While the topics may vary, we offer participants the following experience across all courses:

  • Built-in cross-silo learning. Participants include a wide variety of professionals undertaking social change; they may be based in nonprofit organizations, government, foundations, CSR & social impact programs, or social enterprises.
  • Tools, tactics, resources (plus real-world examples) to help operationalize the abstract concepts of “innovation” and “impact”. We employ a variety of case studies, facilitated exercises, group discussions and break-out sessions.
  • Improved communication across sectors, including a shared understanding of commonly used terminology (breaking down buzzwords).
  • Tailored support. Space is limited to ensure that we are able to customize each bootcamp to program participants.
